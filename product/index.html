Fashyfi Core Features

High-performance e-commerce tools designed to scale operations.

Product & Stock Sync

Monitor and categorize all platform products in real-time. System tracks total stock levels with automated alerts for low stock (e.g. 1 item left) or out of stock items.

Unified Order Tracking

Trace customer orders from dispatch to delivery. Integrates order statuses like awaiting pickup, processing, and shipped for platforms like Riverbird.

Advanced Analytics

Observe Orders and Revenue trends with visual graphs. Track key platform statistics like total revenue (₹55,071) and order frequency over selected timeframes.

Merchant KYC & Wallet

Provides vendors with a dedicated wallet showing available balances and withdrawal histories. Built-in KYC system verifies GST and PAN details instantly.
